Welcome Note

1st Al Ain Regional Pharmacy Conference

We are delighted to extend a warm welcome to the 1st Al Ain Region Pharmacy Conference, taking place on December 6th to 7th, 2025, in the beautiful city of Al Ain. Known for its unique charm and rich heritage, Al Ain provides a perfect setting for valuable professional exchange.

This conference is designed to engage, inspire and promote the exchange of ideas around the most recent developments in pharmaceutical and clinical research. Through insightful discussions, collaborative learning and expert-led sessions, we aim to enhance pharmaceutical knowledge, strengthen pharmacy-related skills and foster an environment where evidence-based practice continues to thrive.

We sincerely encourage all of you to seize this opportunity to actively participate, share knowledge, and contribute to the collective pursuit to advance in pharmacy education and patient care.

On behalf of the 1st Al Ain Region Pharmacy Conference Scientific and Organizing committee members, we extend our sincerest gratitude to the organizers, sponsors, speakers, and attendees for their dedication and support in making this event possible.
